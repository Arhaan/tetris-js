Hello! This is a classic game of Tetris.

The controls are:

Left or Right (or A or D): Move the tetramino to the side

Down (or S): Increase the downward speed of the tetramino while pressed

Up (or W, or MouseClick on the Board): Rotate the tetramino

Bomb:

Click on Bomb to freeze screen and select a square. All the blocks in the 3x3 grid around the square gets destroyed. USing Bomb Feature costs 50 points
