Hello! This is a classic game of Tetris.

The controls are:

Left or Right (or A or D): Move the tetramino to the side

Down (or S): Increase the downward speed of the tetramino while pressed

Up (or W, or MouseClick on the Board): Rotate the tetramino

Bomb:

Click on bomb to freeze screen and select a square. All the blocks in the 3x3 grid around the square get destroyed. Using Bomb costs 50 points

The level changes according to your score. Different levels have different game speeds and points multiplier

Level 1 begins at 700 points

Level 2 begins at 2500 points

Level 3 begins at 6000 points

Level 4 begins at 12000 points
